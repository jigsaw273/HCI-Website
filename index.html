Human Computer Interaction

What is Human Computer Interaction?

Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) is the study of how humans interact with computers. It encompasses a set of principles that make computer design more usable, intuitive, and accessible to humans. Whether you are a software developer or designer, having knowledge about HCI can significantly enhance the user experience of your projects. As technology continues to advance, the need for thoughtful and deliberate computer design is more crucial that ever
